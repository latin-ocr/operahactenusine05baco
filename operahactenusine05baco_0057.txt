Chapter III on marvellous plants (pp. 119-21) has an interesting
account of the action of the planets on vegetable life. It is somewhat
noteworthy that in the Arabic text the principle of fluidity is attributed
to Jupiter, contrary to the whole scheme of astrological theory.
'Philip restores it to Mercury.
