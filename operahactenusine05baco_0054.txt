From this point to the end of the section is the famous Emerald
Table of Hermes, which appears here in its earliest known form. It
has every appearance of considerable antiquity and is probably of
Egyptian origin, passing through Byzantine treatiseS. There are
several distinct translations of it current, but this is probably the best,
as it is, on the whole, the simplest. The Arabic text appears to have
received some additions. q
